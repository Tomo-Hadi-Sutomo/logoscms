FOR INSTALLATION:
1. UNZIP the logosCMS file, place them on your /htdocs or /public_html folder on your webserver
2. Go to your MYSQL browser (phpMyAdmin, SQLBuddy, etc), and create USERNAME and PASSWORD for mysql access, then create DATABASE!
3. Change mysql USERNAME, PASSWORD and DATABASE within CONFIG.PHP file based on your setting above!
4. Change TABLE for mysql table too as your desire.
5. Don't Forget to change your ADMIN USERNAME and PASSWORD for security!
6. Open your logosCMS website in your browser.
7. Just It!, You are Successfully Install logosCMS on your webserver.
8. Don't forget to remove SCRIPT AT LINE 2 at INDEX.PHP file and DELETE INSTALL.PHP file!
